39% of Kashmir's population has COVID-19 antibodies: Sero survey

A sero survey conducted in Kashmir has found that 39% of the population has developed antibodies against COVID-19. The highest prevalence of antibodies is in Pulwama (43.1%) and the lowest in Kulgam (28.5%), Muhammad Salim Khan, head of community medicine at Government Medical College said. The prevalence of antibodies is more among females (40.2%) than males (37.3%), the survey revealed.
